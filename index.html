Times Food & Nightlife Awards

20 years, 11 Cities, 2000+ nominated restaurants and nightlife venues, 1.8 Million+ public votes, 10 Million+ Social media reach, 700+ awarded venues a year, a galaxy of celebrities, socialites, high fashion and the best food and nightlife in the country held in 5 star properties around the nation.

The Times Food and Nightlife Awards – A Times of India Group Company, celebrating and awarding culinary excellence!

Awards Model

The only food awards in the country that combines critical opinion and public voting!

Panel of critics

We have a panel of renowned food and nightlife critics who throughout the year rate and review restaurants and nightlife venues, and selecting the best of these as the nominees for the current year!

Best of the Best

Combining the critic panel vote, influencer and public votes via a weighted average system ensures the final winner is the best of both worlds – critical and popular!

Public and Influencers

Voting lines are opened for 30 days per city for public voting and also a panel of food and lifestyle influencers vote for their favorites
